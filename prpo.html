Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Myline Media (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and discloses information in connection with FISHKY (the “App”), a mobile game available on Google Play and, where applicable, other app stores.

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Policy, please do not use the App.

1. Audience and Scope

The App is a casual puzzle game intended for a general audience of teenagers and adults. The App does not contain violence, sexual content, or other content unsuitable for a general audience, and it is not directed to, marketed to, or designed for children. In our Google Play “Target audience and content” declaration, FISHKY is not designated as primarily directed to children under 13, and Google Play’s Families Policy and Designed for Families program requirements do not apply to the App.

We comply with applicable privacy laws, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (“GDPR”), the UK GDPR, and the California Consumer Privacy Act as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (“CCPA/CPRA”). Section 5 below also describes our approach in the event the App is incidentally used by children, consistent with the U.S. Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (“COPPA”).

2. Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account to use the App, and the App does not directly ask you to provide personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

However, the App is supported by advertising delivered through Unity LevelPlay, an advertising mediation platform that connects the App to multiple third-party advertising networks (“Advertising Partners”). To deliver, measure, and improve advertising, Unity LevelPlay and our Advertising Partners may automatically collect the following information from your device when you use the App:

  • Advertising identifier (e.g., the Google Advertising ID / AAID, or, on devices where it has been limited or reset, a non-identifying placeholder)
  • IP address and approximate location derived from it (e.g., country or region)
  • Device information, such as device model, operating system and version, language and locale settings, screen resolution, and time zone
  • App usage and interaction data, such as which ads are shown, viewed, clicked, or closed, and general gameplay/session information relevant to ad delivery
  • Diagnostic and crash data used to maintain app stability

This information is processed by Unity and our Advertising Partners in accordance with their own privacy policies, as described in Section 4 below. We do not control, and are not responsible for, the specific data practices of these third parties beyond the configuration options we make available to them.

3. How We Use Information

Information described in Section 2 is used to:

  • Display and, where permitted, personalize in-app advertisements
  • Measure advertising performance, attribution, and analytics
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, abuse, and security issues
  • Maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the App’s functionality and stability

4. Advertising Partners and Third-Party SDKs

The App uses Unity LevelPlay to mediate advertising demand from multiple certified third-party ad networks. Depending on your region, device, and app version, these may include (without limitation):

  • Unity Ads
  • Google AdMob
  • Meta Audience Network
  • AppLovin
  • Liftoff / Vungle
  • Other LevelPlay-certified mediation partners, which may change from time to time

Each of these partners independently collects and processes data under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review the following resources for more information:

  • Unity Privacy Policy: unity.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Google AdMob / Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Meta Audience Network Data Policy: facebook.com/privacy/policy
  • AppLovin Privacy Policy: applovin.com/privacy
  • Liftoff / Vungle Privacy Policy: liftoff.io/privacy-policy

This list may not be exhaustive and may change as our mediation configuration changes. Myline Media: review and update this list periodically to match the networks actually active in your LevelPlay configuration and your completed Google Play Data Safety form.

5. Children’s Privacy

FISHKY is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the relevant minimum age of digital consent in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. As noted in Section 1, the App is not designated in Google Play as primarily directed to children, and the Families Policy does not apply.

If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected information from a child in a manner inconsistent with applicable law, we will take steps to delete such information as soon as reasonably possible.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has used the App and provided us with personal information, please contact us using the details in Section 10 so that we can address the matter.

6. Your Choices and Rights

Advertising Identifier

You can reset, delete, or limit ad personalization based on your device’s advertising identifier at any time through your device settings (typically under Settings > Privacy > Ads, or Settings > Google > Ads, depending on your Android version).

Users in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, our Advertising Partners (via Unity LevelPlay’s consent tools) will request your consent before using your data for personalized advertising, in accordance with the GDPR, UK GDPR, and applicable ePrivacy rules. You can change or withdraw your consent at any time through the consent/privacy options presented within the App.

California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you may have the right to know what categories of personal information are collected about you, to request deletion of certain information, and to opt out of the “sale” or “sharing” of personal information for cross-context behavioral (targeted) advertising. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 10, and/or use the device-level advertising controls described above.

Other Jurisdictions

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights regarding your personal information under local law. Please contact us using the details in Section 10 if you wish to exercise any such rights.
